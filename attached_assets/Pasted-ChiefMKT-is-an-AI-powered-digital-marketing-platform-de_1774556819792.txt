ChiefMKT is an AI-powered digital marketing platform designed for any business that has a website and wants to grow online — without needing a full marketing team or agency.
The core idea is simple: instead of juggling 10 different tools (one for SEO, one for analytics, one for email, one for social, etc.), ChiefMKT brings everything under one roof and puts an AI brain on top of all of it. That AI acts like a Chief Marketing Officer — it watches what's happening on your site, spots opportunities, and either tells you what to do or does it for you.
What it actually does:
You drop a small piece of code on your website — one snippet — and from that moment ChiefMKT starts tracking every visitor, every click, every scroll, every lead. It knows who's on your site right now, where they came from, and what they're doing.
On top of that tracking layer, there are 19 tools built in. An SEO analyzer that reads your pages and tells you exactly what's broken and how to fix it. A keyword research tool that finds what your customers are actually searching for. A heatmap that shows where people click and where they drop off. A funnel analyzer that shows you exactly where you're losing people in your sales process. An A/B testing tool. A content generator for blog posts, ad copy, social media, and email sequences. A lead intelligence system that scores every visitor and tells you who's worth chasing.
There's also a chat widget that sits on your site and talks to visitors — qualifies them, handles objections, captures their email — powered by AI, running 24/7 without you touching it.
The Browser Co-Pilot is a separate layer that helps your sales team as they browse the web — analyzing competitor websites and drafting personalized outreach messages on the spot.
Who it's for:
Any business with an online presence. Whether you're a solo founder running a SaaS product, an ecommerce store, a local service business, or a marketing agency managing multiple clients — ChiefMKT gives you the kind of marketing intelligence that used to require an entire team of specialists.
The business model:
Subscription-based. You pay monthly, get access to all the tools, and the AI gets smarter about your specific business the longer you use it. There are tiers from a basic starter plan all the way to an agency plan where you can white-label the whole platform and run it under your own brand for your clients.
The big picture:
Most marketing tools are passive — they show you data and leave you to figure out what to do with it. ChiefMKT is active. It doesn't just report that your bounce rate is high, it tells you why, shows you which page is causing it, generates the copy to fix it, and offers to run an A/B test to prove the fix works.
